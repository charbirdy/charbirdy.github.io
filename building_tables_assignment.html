3 Simple Ingredients for a Cookie
Classic Cookie Recipe
Item How Much The Cost
Butter 2 sticks $2.75
Sugar Half a cup $3.50
Flour 2 cups $4.00
  1. In a bowl, mix the butter and sugar until it is soft
  2. Add the flour until it becomes a dough-like texture
  3. Roll the dough into balls and press down. Put them on parchment paper
  4. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees fahrenheit and bake for 12 minutes. Then, let it cool
